Building Landing Pages

Landing pages are an crucial part of digital marketing. A landing page is intended to maximise the conversion of a visitor towards a lead or sale. They make it easy to generate leads.

Landing Page Goals

We build effective landing pages that meet their objectives.

Register

Get a registration; typically to generate a lead which leads ultimately to sale.

Profile

Profile and qualify the site visitor in order to deliver more relevant follow-up marketing communications.

Add Value

Explain the value proposition offered by the company to differentiate from other sites the visitor may visit during the buying process.

Communicate

Communicate the brand values.

Get Extra Details

Get additional details after the initial sign up.

Types of Landing Page

We have to be aware that there are different types of landing pages that work best depending on the campaign objectives and whether it is a short-term or long-term campaign. Whatever your campaign objectives are. Whether it is a short-term or long-term campaign we can deliver:

Landing pages integrated into current website and style
Bespoke landing pages that are not part of the main site structure or style
Microsites with several pages

Landing Page Success Factors

An effective landing page needs to combine usability, accessibility, persuasion and develop trust in the brand.

Deliver Relevance

The first thing you have to do is instantly show relevance to help visitors achieve that goal.

Integrate

In terms of message, branding and creative the landing page needs to deliver an integrated communication.

Provide Detail

The whole experience and content needs to be right to generate response. If there isn't enough detailed information the visitor can decide to not sign up.

The User Journey

Minimise the number of clicks required for response since every extra click required in response will generally reduce response by 10%.

Page Length

Make the page length short for impulsive readers but long for readers who want to read more.

Graphics

Don't underestimate the importance of quality graphics. They must be consistent with the campaign.
